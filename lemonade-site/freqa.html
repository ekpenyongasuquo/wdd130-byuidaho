Who would benefit from drinking Lemonade Juice?

We think the ‘healthful carbonated’ drinks on the market today are simply not as appealing in taste as regular carbonated drinks. Level Lemonade has been carefully engineered for consumers after a ‘cravable’ lemonade that is actually good for you!

Why low sugar and not sugar free?

Our drink provides half of the Recommended Dietary Intake (RDI) of magnesium* a total of 160mg of magnesium in one bottle. This is important because about half of all Australians don't get enough magnesium in their diet. If you find you are tired throughout the day, or anxious, have sore muscles, suffer from mental fog or maybe headaches and migraines the daily dose of Level Lemonade may help as these are all signs of having an inadequate amount of magnesium.

Can you overdose on magnesium?

A magnesium overdose is called hyper-magnesia. Hyper-magnesia can occur from taking too much magnesium supplement, however this is extremely rare because the kidneys work to get rid of excess magnesium.**Doses less than 410 mg of magnesium are considered safe for most adults. ** Lemonade Juice contains 160 mg..
