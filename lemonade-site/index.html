How many servings can you have per day?

Level Lemonade is a Formulated Supplementary Sports Food supplying half of the RDI of magnesium and Vitamin C, and should be consumed once daily and not in conjunction with other magnesium and Vitamin C supplements.This beverage is not a sole source of nutrition and should be consumed in conjunction with a nutritious diet and an appropriate physical training or exercise program.
