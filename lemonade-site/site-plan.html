Overview

Purpose

Lemonade Juice Company, was founded in 2021 by Asuquo Mfon, a long serving “Fast Moving Consumer Goods” enthusiast from Calabar.Under his leadership our small team were convinced that there is a chance to establish a beverage start up by focussing on drinks that are “better for you”. Since then we have successfully established ourselves through our exclusive distribution agreements with natural iced tea and energy drink brands: Calabar-Beverage and Five-Star. Since the establishment of our import/distribution business, we have been working on daring to establish our very own beverage. After years of investigation, we had decided to focus our efforts on creating a new kind of lemonade. Our team loves a lemon flavoured carbonated soft drink – but felt that current offerings lacked sophistication and were too high in sugar whilst lacking in additional health benefits. Those who had “better for you” propositions, simply did not taste good enough to sway a “die hard” carbonated soft drink endorser.

We seized the opportunity to engage with multiple nutritional experts and product developers in our search for a “new lemonades Juice. Here the very foundations that Lemonade Juice was built upon surfaced: A tasty “crave-able” carbonated soft drink With low sugar (97.5% free) And additional benefits (High Magnesium and Vitamin C) But we did not want to distract from the fact that first and foremost it is a soft-drink: Something you would want to grab from a fridge on a hot day – not even necessarily thinking about the additional benefits but rather enjoying the thirst quenching experience. Through 5+ years of dealing with many trade partners in Nigeria, we are in the fortunate position to be granted an opportunity to present our newly created portfolio to a vast array of sales professionals. We hope it gives Lemonade Juice fighting chance it deserves to gain sufficient distribution to allow broader availability in the market place!Whilst a daring adventure lies ahead – we are quietly confident that the overwhelmingly positive responses we receive from our consumers are a representation of the great potential Lemonade Juice has!

Home page

Our Background: Over the years, we have shared our favorite beverage recipes with our family and friends. When we decided to open Lemonade Juice Company, we knew we wanted to produce a premium, low-sugar product using high-quality ingredients. It is important for us to support our local businesses by purchasing ingredients from our local farmers and other local suppliers. Our Process: Our ingredients are sourced locally. When combined with filtered water, real lemon juice, a dash of sugar and infused with local fruits like NB cranberries or NS blueberries, the result is a delicious, high-quality beverage we were proud to serve to family and friends. Why Lemonade Juice Company? We wanted a name that captured the essence of the unexpected flavors that we infuse into our products to create a delicious and memorable experience.
